Who We Are

Institutional Discipline for Sustainable Public Market Success

Moneyfest Capital Advisors is a corporate advisory firm partnering with promoters, founders, and management teams to strengthen governance, optimize capital structures, and prepare businesses for institutional capital, strategic transactions and public market opportunities.

We bridge the gap between traditional family-run corporate architectures and the standards expected by institutional investors, lenders, merchant bankers and public market stakeholders. Our approach combines strategic clarity with execution discipline to help businesses scale responsibly.

Our advisory practice is designed to support ambitious companies that are ready to move from founder-led growth to institutionally credible enterprise value creation.
